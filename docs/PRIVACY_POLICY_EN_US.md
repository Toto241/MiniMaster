# Privacy Policy for MiniMaster — United States

**Version:** 2026.04.22-1  
**Last Updated:** 2026-04-22  
**Jurisdiction:** United States of America  
**Status:** Technical template — requires legal review before production use.

> **Note:** This policy addresses COPPA (children under 13), CCPA/CPRA (California), VCDPA (Virginia), CPA (Colorado), and general US privacy law. State-specific supplements may be required.

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to MiniMaster. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile applications (MasterApp and ChildApp) and web services in the United States.

## 2. Information We Collect

### Personal Data
- **Parent/Guardian Information:** Email address and unique app-scoped device identifier.
- **Child Information:** Unique app-scoped device identifier for each paired child device.

### Derivative Data
- **Usage Data:** Apps blocked, time limits set, tasks assigned.
- **Device Information:** Mobile device ID, model, manufacturer.

### Financial Data
We do not collect or store financial information. Subscription payments are processed through Google Play Store.

## 3. COPPA Compliance (Children Under 13)

MiniMaster complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):
1. We do **not** knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13.
2. All accounts are created by parents/guardians. Child device identifiers are collected only with **verifiable parental consent**.
3. Parents may review, delete, or refuse further collection of their child's data at any time by contacting [privacy@minimaster.app](mailto:privacy@minimaster.app).
4. We do not condition participation on disclosure of more information than is reasonably necessary.
5. Our data retention policy for child information is minimal and tied to active account use.

## 4. How We Use Your Information

- Create and manage your account.
- Provide core services (device locking, app blocking, task management).
- Send push notifications regarding task status.
- Monitor and analyse usage trends.
- Notify you of app updates.

## 5. Disclosure of Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information for promotional purposes.

We may share information:
- **By Law or to Protect Rights:** To respond to legal process or protect rights, property, and safety.
- **Service Providers:** With third parties that perform services for us (data analysis, email delivery, hosting, customer service). We use Firebase (Google) for backend services; the [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) applies.

## 6. State Privacy Rights

Depending on your state of residence, you may have the following rights:

### California (CCPA/CPRA)
- Right to know what personal information is collected, used, shared, or sold.
- Right to delete personal information.
- Right to correct inaccurate personal information.
- Right to opt out of sale or sharing of personal information.
- Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights.
- **We do not "sell" personal information as defined by CCPA.**
- To exercise CCPA rights, email [ccpa@minimaster.app](mailto:ccpa@minimaster.app) or call our toll-free number.

### Virginia (VCDPA), Colorado (CPA), Connecticut (CTDPA), Utah (UCPA)
- Right to access, correct, delete, and obtain a copy of personal data.
- Right to opt out of targeted advertising, sale, and profiling.
- To exercise these rights, email [privacy@minimaster.app](mailto:privacy@minimaster.app).

## 7. Security of Your Information

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures. Despite our efforts, no security measures are perfect or impenetrable.

## 8. Data Retention and Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. We will process your request within a reasonable timeframe. Email [privacy@minimaster.app](mailto:privacy@minimaster.app).

## 9. Support Access

You may voluntarily grant our support team **temporary, time-limited (48 hours), revocable, and logged** access to your account data for troubleshooting. Without your explicit consent, our support team has **no access**.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be notified in-app or by email with at least 30 days' notice.
