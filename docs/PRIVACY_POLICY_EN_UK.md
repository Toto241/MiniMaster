# Privacy Policy for MiniMaster — United Kingdom

**Version:** 2026.04.22-1  
**Last Updated:** 2026-04-22  
**Jurisdiction:** United Kingdom  
**Status:** Technical template — requires legal review before production use.

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to MiniMaster. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile applications (MasterApp and ChildApp) and web services in the United Kingdom. Please read this policy carefully.

## 2. Data Controller

MiniMaster Operations is the data controller for the purposes of UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

- **Email:** [privacy@minimaster.app](mailto:privacy@minimaster.app)
- **ICO Register:** We are registered with the Information Commissioner's Office.

## 3. Information We Collect

### Personal Data
- **Parent/Guardian Information:** Email address and unique app-scoped device identifier.
- **Child Information:** Unique app-scoped device identifier for each paired child device.

### Derivative Data
- **Usage Data:** Apps blocked, time limits set, tasks assigned.
- **Device Information:** Mobile device ID, model, manufacturer.

### Financial Data
We do not collect or store financial information. All subscription payments are processed through Google Play Store.

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing (UK GDPR)

We process personal data under the following lawful bases:
1. **Contract (Art. 6(1)(b) UK GDPR):** To provide the app services.
2. **Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) UK GDPR):** For optional features and analytics.
3. **Legitimate Interests (Art. 6(1)(f) UK GDPR):** For security, debugging, and fraud prevention.
4. **Legal Obligation (Art. 6(1)(c) UK GDPR):** Where required by law.

## 5. Age-Appropriate Design (AADC)

MiniMaster is designed for parental use. Children under 18 do not create independent accounts. We apply the UK Age Appropriate Design Code standards:
- High privacy defaults.
- No profiling of children.
- No behavioural advertising.
- Data minimisation.

## 6. How We Use Your Information

- Create and manage your account.
- Provide core services (device locking, app blocking, task management).
- Send push notifications regarding task status.
- Monitor and analyse usage trends.
- Notify you of app updates.

## 7. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed on servers outside the UK (e.g., Firebase/Google Cloud in the US or EU). We ensure that:
1. Appropriate safeguards under UK GDPR Article 46 are in place (UK Standard Contractual Clauses).
2. A Transfer Risk Assessment (TRA) has been conducted.
3. Supplementary technical measures (encryption) are applied where necessary.

## 8. Your Data Subject Rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:
- **Access** your personal data (Art. 15).
- **Rectification** of inaccurate data (Art. 16).
- **Erasure** ("right to be forgotten") (Art. 17).
- **Restriction** of processing (Art. 18).
- **Data portability** (Art. 20).
- **Object** to processing (Art. 21).
- **Complain** to the ICO ([ico.org.uk](https://ico.org.uk)).

## 9. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data only as long as necessary for the purposes set out in this policy or as required by law. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing [privacy@minimaster.app](mailto:privacy@minimaster.app).

## 10. Security

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures. No security measure is perfect, and no data transmission method is guaranteed against interception.

## 11. Support Access

For troubleshooting, you may voluntarily grant our support team **temporary access** (48 hours, revocable, fully logged) to your account data. Without your explicit consent, our support team has **no access**.

## 12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified in-app or by email, and renewed consent may be sought where required.
